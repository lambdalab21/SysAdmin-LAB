## What counts as understanding

A chapter is not finished until he can answer:

1. What problem does this chapter solve?
2. What file, command, or warning matters?
3. What should be skipped for now?
4. What beginner mistake does this prevent?
5. What evidence would I inspect on `app01` or `db01`?

Highlighting is not studying. Recognizing a command is not understanding it. SSH is a career-long tool; sloppy habits here become expensive later.
